### 📦 Méthodes de Déploiement

- #### <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Koyeb-grey?style=for-the-badge&logo=koyeb&logoColor=white" height="28" /> <br> [![Deploy to Koyeb](https://www.koyeb.com/static/images/deploy/button.svg)](https://app.koyeb.com/deploy?type=git&name=menma-md&repository=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FDr-Djibi%2FDeploie-menma-md&branch=main&builder=dockerfile&instance_type=free) <br>

- #### <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Render-grey?style=for-the-badge&logo=render&logoColor=white" height="28" /> <br> [![Deploy to Render](https://render.com/images/deploy-to-render-button.svg)](https://render.com/deploy?repo=https://bitbucket.org/dr-djibi/public_dockerfile_menma) <br>

- #### <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Panel-grey?style=for-the-badge&logo=windows-terminal&logoColor=white" height="28" /> <br> [![Télécharger Menma-MD (ZIP)](https://img.shields.io/badge/Télécharger_Menma_MD_(ZIP)-1E232E?style=for-the-badge&logo=windows-terminal&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/Dr-Djibi/Menma-MD/archive/refs/heads/main.zip)
- Extrayez l'archive et importez tous les fichiers sur votre Panel.
- Créez un fichier `.env` à la racine ou configurez vos variables d'environnement dans l'onglet **Startup** ou **Files** de votre Panel.
- Démarrez simplement la console en lançant le bot via le bouton **Start** de votre Panel ou `npm start` ou `node index.js` .
